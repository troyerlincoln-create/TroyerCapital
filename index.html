Why a Fixed Indexed Annuity

Your Retirement,
Secured & Growing

A Fixed Indexed Annuity (FIA) gives you the growth potential of the market with a contractual guarantee that you will never lose a dollar of principal — built for people who can't afford to lose what they've saved.

0% Floor — Guaranteed

No matter what the market does, your account never loses value. Principal is protected by contract.

Index-Linked Growth

Credits are tied to indexes like the S&P 500. When the market rises, your account grows — when it falls, you stay flat.

Tax-Deferred Growth

Your money compounds without being taxed each year — you only pay taxes when you withdraw, giving savings more time to grow.

Guaranteed Lifetime Income

Optional income riders provide a paycheck you cannot outlive — true retirement security regardless of market conditions.
